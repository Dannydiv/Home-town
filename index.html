Lugbe Community is categorized into three section

1. The Famous Federal Housing (FHA)

Coming from the Airport, FHA is located to the left hand side, and it span accross all the four bus-stop in lugbe from Police sign post, the berger-yard, Car-wash down to the Federal housing bus-stop

2. Lugbe Accross

The Rural Unathorized Settlement also known as "Lugbe Accross" and it is divided into zones, zones 1 through 9.

3. Lugbe Estates

At the back of Lugbe Accross as it is famously called are the various Estate owned and developed by individuals, these Estates house some the middle class citizen and perhaps some rich individuals who for one reasons or the others do not want to stay in Maitama and Asokoro.

Major Economic Activities in Lugbe

POS Business

A POS business of the major economic activities people easily engaged in Nigeria and Lugbe area is not an exception, this is largely due to rural populations, isolated settlements, and lack of access to banks.

A Private Nursery and Primary School Business in Lugbe.

This business is a go-to business for every enterpreneur because of its high profit due to the sheer number of children. With such increase in the number of children born yearly - comes the need for more nursery anf primary schools to cater for them.

Okada Riding Business

Okada riding is another lucrative business youth engaged in due to the facts that it is easy to start, it's the only source of transportation from the bus-stop to the commnunity, the bad road condition made it difficult and almost impossible for cars to drive, even the cars owners mostly park their cars at the open places near the road (airport road) and will order a bike to take them to their respective homes and this okada riding business required no formal education so it is go-to occupation for timing growing population of unskilled workers in the rural area.
